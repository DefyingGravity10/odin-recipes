Pesto Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs is amongst the classic breakfast recipes known to mankind. This dish is simple and easy-to-prepare. However, its greatest charm is that it can be created with various ingredients depending on the tastes of the individual.

This version of the Pesto Scrambled Eggs is conceptualized by WADESOPHIA. The recipe entails simple ingredients which are accessible in most households. The one written below can be prepared in around 10 minutes and could yield to one serving.

Ingredients

  • 1 lightly beaten egg
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon pesto
  • 1/4 cup shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat some oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Place the egg, Cheddar cheese, salt, and pepper in a small bowl then mix them well.
  3. Pour the mixture into the pan and stir this for 3 to 5 minutes, or until desired texture is obtained.
  4. Remove the cooked eggs from the pan and mix it in pesto.

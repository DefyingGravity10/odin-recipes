Cheesy Hashbrown Casserole

Description

This version of the Cheesy Hashbrown Casserole is conceptualized by CMANSKY. The recipe includes potatoes, Cheddar cheese and a crunchy cornflake topping which will satisfy the tastebuds of potato lovers.

The overall dish can be created in one hour and can yield to one 3-quart casserole. This is equivalent to 12 servings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of frozen hash browns (2 pounds, thawed)
  • 2 cups of shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 can of condensed cream of chicken soup (10.75 oz)
  • 8 oz of sour cream
  • 3/4 cup of melted butter
  • 1/2 cup of chopped onion
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 2 cups of crushed cornflakes

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (175 degrees Celsius)
  2. Place the hash browns, Cheddar cheese, condensed soup, sour cream, 1/2 cup melted butter, onion, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Transfer mixture to a 3-quart casserole dish.
  3. Saute cornflakes and the remaining 1/4 cup melted butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Sprinkle the mixture over the top of the casserole.
  4. Cover casserole and bake in the oven for 40 minutes.
